Name: (0) Kadhai Paneer
Cuisine: Indian
Category: Main Course
Difficulty: Medium
PrepTime: 20
CookTime: 25
Ingredients: paneer: 250 gm,tomatoes: 3 pc,bell peppers: 2 pc,onions: 2 pc,ginger-garlic paste: 1 tbsp,kadhai masala: 2 tsp,red chili powder: 1 tsp,coriander powder: 1 tsp,garam masala: ½ tsp,fresh cream: 2 tbsp,oil: 2 tbsp,salt: to taste
Description: A popular North Indian dish made with soft paneer cubes and a blend of aromatic spices, cooked in a thick tomato-based gravy with bell peppers and onions.
Instructions: \n1) Heat oil in a kadhai and lightly sauté paneer cubes until golden. \n2) Set aside. \n3) In the same kadhai, heat oil, add ginger-garlic paste, and sauté for a minute. \n4) Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. \n5) Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.\n6) Add kadhai masala, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well. \n7) Toss in chopped bell peppers and cook for 5-7 minutes. Add sautéed paneer, garam masala, and fresh cream. \n8) Cook for another 5 minutes. Serve hot with naan or roti.

Cuisine: Indian
Category: Dessert
Difficulty: Medium
PrepTime: 15
CookTime: 30
Ingredients: milk: 1 liter,lemon juice: 2 tbsp,sugar: 1 cup,water: 4 cups
Description: A classic Indian sweet made of soft, spongy cheese balls soaked in a light sugar syrup.
Instructions: \n1) Boil milk in a pan, add lemon juice, and stir until the milk curdles. \n2)Strain the curdled milk using a muslin cloth to separate the whey.\n3) Wash the chenna under cold water to remove acidity. \n4)Knead the chenna until smooth, then make small balls without cracks. In a deep pan, boil water with sugar to prepare the syrup. \n5)Gently drop the chenna balls into the boiling syrup and cook for 15 minutes.\n6) Turn off the heat and let the rasgullas cool in the syrup.
